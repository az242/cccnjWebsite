Why We Give

Giving to our Christian church is a profound expression of our faith and commitment. It symbolizes our devotion to God and the values that guide our lives. It is an act of selflessness, a testament to our desire to support our community, and a way to carry out the teachings of Jesus, who emphasized the importance of love, compassion, and generosity.

Our contributions help sustain the church, enabling it to provide a place of worship, spiritual guidance, and support to those in need. It's not just a financial offering; it's a heartfelt dedication to the spiritual home that nurtures our souls and a recognition of the vital role it plays in our lives.

Giving to our Christian church holds great significance, as it underscores our collective commitment to spreading God's love, fostering fellowship, and strengthening our faith journey.

Via Check

To give via Check, please send your offering with the following details

  • Fill out a personal check with the amount you wish to donate. Make the check payable to CCCNJ
  • Send the check by mail to
    CCCNJ (Attn: FD B04)
    232 South Beverwyck Rd,
    Parsippany, NJ 07054
Zelle

To give via Zelle, please send your offering with the following details

  • Recipient's Email giving@cccnj.org
  • Recipient's Name CCCNJ
  • Message to Recipient include your Name, Offering ID (or address), and fund(s).
Stock/Fund Donation

To give via Stocks/Funds, please send your offering with the following details

  • Fill Out a stock contributions form
  • Send the Form to your financial advisor/broker to begin the process of transferring stocks
  • Notify the treasurer that your donation has completed
  • Receive receipt via mail in January
Employer Matching

Many companies promote charitable donations by providing employer matching to employees

  • Ask if your company allows donation matching and initiate the process
  • Verify CCCNJ is registered as a eligible charity organization in your company
