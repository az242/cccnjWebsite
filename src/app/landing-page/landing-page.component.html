About Us

We are a non-denominational, Bible-based church located in Parsippany, NJ. We conduct Sunday worship services in three languages: English, Mandarin, and Taiwanese. We run Sunday School classes for all ages, many youth and children programs, vocational bible study camps, missionary activities and many more so we can grow stronger in our faith.

We invite you to explore our website and hope that you will come and visit us to worship side by side as a family, and feel the warmth and blessing of Jesus Christ. We are here for you with an open heart, please feel free to leave a comment in the “Contact Us” page if you have any questions. Thank you for visiting our church. All are welcome.
