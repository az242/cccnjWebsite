About Us

We are a non-denominational, Bible-based church located in Parsippany, NJ. We conduct Sunday worship services in three languages: English, Mandarin, and Taiwanese. We run Sunday School classes for all ages, many youth and children programs, vocational bible study camps, missionary activities and many more so we can grow stronger in our faith.

We invite you to explore our website and hope that you will come and visit us to worship side by side as a family, and feel the warmth and blessing of Jesus Christ. We are here for you with an open heart, please feel free to leave a comment in the “Contact Us” page if you have any questions. Thank you for visiting our church. All are welcome.

Give back

Giving back to the church, whether through financial donations or dedicated service, is a powerful way to strengthen one's faith and community bonds.

Equally important is the act of service, which offers the gift of time and talent.

By volunteering and actively participating in the church's activities, individuals not only contribute to the community's growth but also deepen their connection with their faith and fellow congregants.

Family Church

Being a Multi-generational church reflects our commitment to creating an inclusive and diverse community where people from various life stages can worship, learn, and grow together.

This approach ensures that wisdom, experiences, and perspectives from different generations are shared, enriching our collective understanding of faith.
